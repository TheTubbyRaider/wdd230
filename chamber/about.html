About the Chamber of Commerce

Welcome to the Orem, Utah Chamber of Commerce! Our chamber is dedicated to fostering a thriving business community in Orem and beyond. Here's what makes us special:

  • Connect: We provide networking opportunities for businesses of all sizes, helping them connect and grow.
  • Support: Our chamber offers various levels of membership with exclusive benefits, ensuring businesses get the support they need.
  • Community: We believe in giving back. Our chamber actively participates in community events and initiatives.

Join us in building a stronger business community. Together, we can achieve more!
