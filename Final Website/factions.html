Rebel Alliance

The Rebel Alliance, formed in response to the Galactic Empire's tyranny, fights for freedom and justice. Comprising diverse species and individuals, they engage in heroic struggles against oppression, with iconic leaders like Princess Leia and Luke Skywalker leading the charge. The Alliance is known for its resilience and the daring exploits of its members, such as the successful destruction of the Death Star.

Galactic Empire

The Galactic Empire, led by Emperor Palpatine and Darth Vader, seeks control through military might. It enforces order with a vast army of Stormtroopers, constructing formidable weapons like the Death Star. Despite its authoritarian rule, the Empire claims to bring stability to the galaxy. The Empire's quest for dominance ultimately leads to large-scale conflicts, including the Battle of Endor where the second Death Star is destroyed.

  • Count Dooku
  • General Grievous
  • Director Krennic
  • Admiral Piett
  • IG-88
  • Death Star Trooper
  • Stormtrooper

Jedi Order

The Jedi Order, an ancient order of Force-sensitive individuals, is dedicated to maintaining peace and justice. Jedi, trained in the ways of the Force, serve as guardians of peace. Unfortunately, the Jedi face challenges, including the rise of the Sith, leading to conflicts such as the Clone Wars. The Jedi's commitment to the Light Side of the Force and their code of ethics guide their actions in the galaxy.

  • Anakin Skywalker
  • Obi-Wan Kenobi
  • Ahsoka Tano

First Order

The First Order emerges from the remnants of the Galactic Empire, aspiring to restore its dominance. Led by Supreme Leader Snoke and Kylo Ren, the First Order crushes opposition ruthlessly. It employs Stormtroopers and powerful weaponry, such as Starkiller Base, to assert control. The First Order's militaristic approach and dedication to the dark side of the Force make it a formidable adversary in the ongoing struggle for galactic dominance.

Bounty Hunters

Bounty Hunters, skilled and resourceful mercenaries, take contracts to capture or eliminate targets for rewards. Operating outside traditional allegiances, bounty hunters like Boba Fett and Cad Bane are known for their cunning tactics and ruthless pursuit of their prey. The diverse group of bounty hunters each has a unique skill set, making them formidable adversaries in the criminal underworld.
