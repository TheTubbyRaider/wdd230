Site Name: Orem Chamber of Commerce

The site name was selected to represent our local town and its business community.

Site Purpose:

The Orem Chamber of Commerce website will serve two main purposes:

  1. Promoting local businesses and attracting new ones to Orem.
  2. Providing valuable information about Orem's history, demographics, and upcoming events.

Scenarios:

  • What events will the Orem Chamber of Commerce be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the Orem Chamber of Commerce's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in Orem in the last five years?

Color Schema:

The color scheme for the Orem Chamber of Commerce website consists of the following:

  • Background: #5d5c61
  • Main Text: #7395ae

Typography:
